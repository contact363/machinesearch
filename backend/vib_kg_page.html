Financing, transport & commissioning from a single source

The VIB-Dannenmaier GmbH & Co. Vertrieb- und Industrie-Beratung KG enables the smooth purchase or sale of your used machine tool. Consulting, financing, transport - with us, the specialists you are looking for, work hand in hand. Save yourself the detour and discover our all-round service:

Consulting

Approach us with your request or offer. We will advise you free of charge based on our knowledge of the industry: Where can you find the right used machine? What alternatives are there? What price should you ask for?

When you buy, we accompany you to inspect the machine in use. Ask workers at the machine about the condition and see for yourself!

If you would like to sell, we would be happy to visit you and inspect the used machine without obligation.

Trade

As an independent intermediary, you buy the used machine tool indirectly through us. Legally secure and for the pre-determined price. No additional costs, no delivery problems, no surprises.

Describe your offer or request to us now. We search and find in our network - so that purchase and sale succeed as quickly as possible.

Service

Our all-round service takes the logistical and bureaucratic burden off your shoulders. We support you with transport: In-house and across borders. Financing: Lease the machine from our partners at favorable conditions.

During commissioning: Our partner companies commission the used machine, including CNC programming, at your site.

About us

The „VIB-Dannenmaier GmbH & Co. Vertrieb- und Industrie-Beratung KG“ is a family business based in Karlsbad. Since 1986 founder Udo Dannenmaier knows lathe manufacturers, turning shops and suppliers. A worldwide network where you are sure to find your next used machine.

Meanwhile 7 employees are searching for used machine tools for metalworking worldwide. Steffen and Ulli Dannenmaier continue the business in the second generation.

Our partners

Our specialty is the machinery trade. We will find the right model for you on the used machinery market.

Our partners take care of the trappings. They help you with everything from financing and transport to bringing in, commissioning and training your personnel.
